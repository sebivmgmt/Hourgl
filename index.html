Definition

What is the Bottomless Hourglass?

Hourgl is the Bottomless Hourglass, an hourglass with no bottom. Its sand keeps falling. It cannot be caught, restored, or reset.

A normal hourglass holds time in a sealed chamber. Flip it, and the sand comes back. Flip it again, and you get another turn. The Bottomless Hourglass removes that promise entirely. There is no chamber waiting at the bottom to catch what falls. Once the sand passes through the center, it is gone, and there is no second hourglass behind it.

That is the entire point. The symbol represents urgency, growth, and the plain reality that time is running out, for everyone, all the time, whether you are watching it or not.

Meaning

What does an hourglass with no bottom mean?

An open-bottom hourglass is not a symbol of endless time. It is closer to the opposite. It means:

  • —

    Time is finite. Nothing about this symbol suggests you get more of it.

  • —

    Lost time cannot be recovered. The sand that already fell is not coming back, no matter how the hourglass is turned.

  • —

    Delay has a cost. Every day spent waiting is sand that already passed through.

  • —

    Growth must happen while time remains. There is no later version of today.

  • —

    It is a warning, not a comfort. The symbol does not promise unlimited chances. It is a reminder that you do not have one.

The Name

Why is it called Hourgl?

Say "hourglass" out loud, then take away the bottom of the word the same way the symbol takes away the bottom of the shape.

HOURGLASS
It's an assless hourgl‑ass. The bottom of the word is missing, just like the bottom of the symbol.

HOUR + GL. The name carries both halves of the idea at once: time, and the luck you'll need to spend it well.

Construction

The symbol

The Hourgl mark is built from two mirrored 7s, stacked directly on top of each other.

  • 01

    One 7, then a second 7 mirrored directly on top of it. Two strokes, stacked and flipped.

  • 02

    The mirrored 7s meet to form the letter A, the first letter of Alexandrino.

  • 03

    The top bar is a straight horizontal line. It represents God, and the belief that He is the reason I made it here.

  • 04

    Seven is the number of luck. Two of them, mirrored, doubles it.

  • 05

    The legs below the center point never close. That open bottom is time escaping, the same sand that can never be caught again.
